Crystal Collector Game

You will be given a Goal Number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a mystery amount to Your Number.

You win by matching Your Number to the Goal Number. You lose if Your Number goes above the Goal Number.

Every time the game restarts, the crystal values change.
