Crystal Collector Game

You will be given a Magic Number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a mystery amount to Your Number.

You win by matching Your Number to the Magic Number. You lose if Your Number goes above the Magic Number.

Every time the Magic Number resets, the crystal values change.
